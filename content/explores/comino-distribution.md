## LINKEDIN POST

Most people visit Comino for the Blue Lagoon — in August, with 4,000 other people.

I hike the full island loop in shoulder season. 8.5 km around the perimeter: watchtower from 1618, abandoned military hospital, cliff edges with no barriers, and two bays where you're likely swimming alone.

The difference between peak season and October on Comino is the difference between a theme park and a nature reserve. Same island. Different experience entirely.

Full guide on followtheflor.com — covering route, timing, what to bring, and why shoulder season changes everything.
