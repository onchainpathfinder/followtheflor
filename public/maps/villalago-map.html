From Rome

~2 hours by car via A25 motorway. Exit Cocullo, then follow SP479 along the Sagittario valley.

The Loop Walk

~6 km, 1.5–2.5 hours. Starts in the village, descends to the lake and hermitage, returns via the gorge path.

Parking

Free parking near the village centre. The trailhead to Lago di San Domenico is signed from the main square.
