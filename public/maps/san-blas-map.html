From Mġarr Ferry

~15 min drive via Nadur. Follow signs to Victoria, then Nadur, then Triq San Blas.

Trail Down

500m, 10-15 min down, 15-20 min back up. Moderate-steep. Wear proper shoes.

Parking

8-10 spots at trailhead. Fills by 10am in summer. Backup: park in Nadur village.
