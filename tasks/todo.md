**Search intent:** Informational — people searching want to understand what DefiLlama is, what data it shows, and how to use it for DeFi research/analysis.

**Primary keyword:** what is DefiLlama
**Secondary keywords:** DefiLlama explained, DefiLlama TVL tracker, DeFi analytics tool, how to use DefiLlama
**LSI/semantic:** total value locked, DeFi dashboard, protocol comparison, chain analytics, yield tracker, stablecoin flows, DEX volume

1. What Is DefiLlama? — intro, free open-source DeFi analytics
2. The Dashboard: What You See First — TVL overview, chain breakdown, protocol rankings
3. TVL: The Metric That Matters Most — what TVL means, why it matters, how to read it
4. Chains: Comparing Ecosystems Side by Side — multi-chain view, market share
5. Stablecoins: Following the Money — stablecoin dashboard, flows, dominance
6. DEX Volume and Yields — trading volume, yield opportunities
7. DefiLlama for Compliance Professionals — risk signals, protocol monitoring

**Competitors to beat:** Generic "what is DefiLlama" posts that just describe the homepage. Our angle: practical use cases for both analysts AND compliance professionals.
