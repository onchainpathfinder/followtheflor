## Platform-by-Platform Strategy

### Instagram (@followtheflor)

**Algorithm priorities (2026):**
- Watch time is #1 ranking factor (confirmed by Mosseri)
- DM shares carry the most weight for engagement
- Saves signal high-value content
- Likes are now the weakest signal
- Reels get up to 10x the distribution of static posts

**Content mix (weekly):**
- 3-4 Reels (primary growth driver)
- 1-2 Carousels (community depth + saves)
- Daily Stories (engagement + connection)
- 1 static post if it's truly beautiful (Malta sunset etc.)

**Optimal posting times (CET/Malta time):**
- Tuesday: 10-11am
- Wednesday: 10-11am
- Thursday: 10-12pm
- Friday: 11am
- Weekend: lower engagement, save for Stories only

**Growth tactics:**
- Use Trial Reels to test hooks with non-followers first
- Reels between 7-30 seconds for maximum completion rate
- First 3 seconds must hook — aim for 60%+ 3-second hold rate
- Reply to every comment within the first hour
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags (not more — dilutes focus)
- Add keywords to name field for search ("Flor | Running in Malta")
- Carousel tip: make slide 1 a bold statement, slide 2 "here's why"

**Content pillars:**
- FLOR RUNS: training clips, race content, swim/hike footage, honest fitness talk
- FLOR EXPLORES: Malta hidden spots, coastal walks, food, local culture
- Lifestyle crossover: morning routines, work-life balance, real talk

**Bio template:**
```
Flor | Running Malta's Hidden Trails
🏃‍♀️ Runner • 🏊 Sea swimmer • 🇲🇹 Malta local
Fierce minds. Bold moves.
🔗 followtheflor.com
```

### TikTok (@followtheflor)

**Algorithm priorities (2026):**
- 70%+ completion rate needed for viral potential (up from 50% in 2024)
- First 3 seconds retention matters more than follower count
- Original content strongly favoured over reposts
- Trending sounds get algorithmic push
- 3-8 hashtags maximum

**Content mix (weekly):**
- 3-5 original TikToks
- 1-2 can be adapted from Instagram Reels (but not watermarked copies)
- Trending sound + original content = best combo

**Optimal posting times (CET/Malta time):**
- Monday-Thursday: 6-10pm
- Sunday: 10am (single best time across the whole week)
- Avoid early morning and late night

**Growth tactics:**
- First 30 days: post daily to trigger algorithm testing of new account
- Keep videos 15-30 seconds for maximum completion rate
- Respond to EVERY comment (algorithm counts repeat views from commenters)
- Use trending sounds — even under original content
- Film in recognisable Malta locations (Blue Lagoon, Valletta, Dingli Cliffs)
- Day-in-the-life format performs extremely well for lifestyle creators

**Content formats that work:**
- "A run in [Malta location] nobody knows about"
- "POV: you wake up at 5am to swim in the Mediterranean"
- "Things I wish I knew before moving to Malta"
- Running tips with text-on-screen
- Quick transitions: workout clothes → office → back to exploring

### X/Twitter (@floronchain)

**Algorithm & community (2026):**
- Crypto Twitter has 15-18 million daily active users
- Crypto-focused accounts average 4.2% engagement (vs 2.9% platform average)
- Threads are the primary growth format
- Quote tweets with hot takes drive engagement
- Consistency + genuine interaction > any hack

**Content mix (daily):**
- 3-5 tweets per day (mix of original, reactions, engagement)
- 2-3 threads per week (deep dives, explainers)
- Quote-tweet breaking crypto/regulation news with Flor's take
- Engage with 20+ accounts in crypto/compliance niche daily

**Optimal posting times (CET/Malta time):**
- Monday-Thursday: 9-11am (morning news cycle)
- Lunchtime: 1-2pm (secondary spike)
- Avoid weekends for important threads

**Growth tactics:**
- First take on breaking regulation news = massive engagement
- Thread format: tweet 1 = hook, tweets 2-7 = value, last tweet = CTA
- Use relevant hashtags sparingly (#MiCA #AML #crypto) — 1-2 per tweet max
- Build relationships with 10-15 key accounts in compliance/crypto space
- Pin your best performing thread to profile
- "Plain English" explainers of complex regulation are underserved and hugely shareable

**Content formats that work:**
- "MiCA explained in 10 tweets"
- "🚩 Red flag thread: [typology] — how to spot it"
- "[Breaking news] → here's what it actually means for you"
- "The [regulation] is coming. Here's your checklist."
- Quick takes on crypto news (1-2 tweets, opinionated)

### LinkedIn (Personal profile, floronchain content)

**Algorithm (2026):**
- Peak engagement has shifted to late afternoon/evening (3-8pm)
- Wednesday 5pm and Friday 3-4pm are top slots
- First-line hook determines whether post gets expanded
- Comments from connections boost distribution significantly

**Content mix (weekly):**
- 2-3 posts per week maximum
- Educational only — regulation updates, compliance insights, industry analysis
- Professional tone — Flor's boss is on LinkedIn

**⚠️ BOSS-SAFE CHECK — run this before every LinkedIn post:**
1. Does it reveal anything about Flor's employer? → NO
2. Could it be interpreted as promoting a competitor? → NO
3. Is it purely educational/industry-focused? → YES
4. Would Flor be comfortable if her CEO read it? → YES
If any check fails, do not post to LinkedIn.

**Optimal posting times (CET/Malta time):**
- Wednesday: 5pm
- Friday: 3-4pm
- Tuesday: 4pm (secondary)

**Growth tactics:**
- Comment thoughtfully on 10+ posts daily in compliance/fintech space
- Never put links in the post body (kills reach) — put link in first comment
- Open with a strong first line (no "I'm excited to share...")
- End with a question to drive comments
- 150-200 words maximum

### YouTube (Flor Onchain)

**Algorithm (2026):**
- Viewer satisfaction beats watch time — short videos with high retention outrank longer ones where viewers drop off
- Channels with consistent upload schedules see 67% faster subscriber growth
- YouTube Shorts get 70+ billion daily views — use as a funnel to long-form
- Thumbnails and titles determine click-through rate

**Content mix (weekly/biweekly):**
- 1 long-form video per week (7-12 minutes) — or every 2 weeks when starting
- 2-3 Shorts per week (repurposed from TikTok/Reels, re-edited without watermarks)
- 90% crypto compliance/regulation, 10% Malta lifestyle crossover

**Optimal posting times (CET/Malta time):**
- Thursday or Friday: 2-4pm (gives algorithm time to test before weekend)
- Shorts: any day, consistency matters more than timing

**Growth tactics:**
- Focus on SEARCHABLE topics for long-form (SEO-driven titles)
- Use vidIQ or TubeBuddy for keyword research before scripting
- Thumbnail: bright colours, text overlay, Flor's face, Malta backdrop
- First 30 seconds must hook — state the problem, promise the solution
- Add timestamps to every long video
- End screen: subscribe + next video suggestion
- Monetisation threshold: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (or 10M Shorts views)

**Content formats that work:**
- "MiCA Explained: What Every Crypto Company Needs to Know"
- "I'm an MLRO — Here's What Actually Triggers a Red Flag"
- "Crypto AML in 2026: The Complete Guide"
- "[Regulation] Breakdown — Plain English, No BS"
- Malta crossover: "Working in Crypto Compliance From Malta" (lifestyle + authority)

## Cross-Posting Rules

| Original Platform | Can Cross-Post To | How |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Reel | TikTok, Facebook, YouTube Shorts | Re-edit without watermark. Adjust caption/hashtags per platform. |
| TikTok | Instagram Reel, YouTube Shorts | Re-edit without watermark. Never cross-post with TikTok logo. |
| X Thread | LinkedIn (condensed), Newsletter | Rewrite for platform tone. LinkedIn = shorter, more professional. |
| YouTube Long | X Thread (key points), Newsletter (summary), Blog post | Extract 2-3 Shorts from each long video. |
| Blog Post | X Thread, LinkedIn, Newsletter, Instagram Carousel | Each blog should produce 4-5 social pieces. |

**Golden rule:** Never cross-post with another platform's watermark. Re-edit or re-export for each platform.
