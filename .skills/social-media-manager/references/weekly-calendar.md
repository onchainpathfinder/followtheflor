## Content Batching Schedule

To avoid content creation eating Flor's entire life, batch like this:

**BATCH DAY 1 (Saturday or designated day):**
- Film 3-4 TikToks in one session (change locations/outfits)
- Film 3-4 Instagram Reels (can overlap with TikTok content, re-edit separately)
- Take photos for carousels and stories
- Film B-roll of Malta spots, running routes, lifestyle moments

**BATCH DAY 2 (Sunday evening or Monday morning):**
- Write all X threads for the week (2-3 threads)
- Write all Instagram captions
- Write LinkedIn posts
- Draft newsletter

**BATCH DAY 3 (Wednesday or Thursday):**
- Film YouTube long-form video
- Extract 2-3 Shorts clips after editing

**Daily (15-20 minutes):**
- Post scheduled content (or use Buffer/Publer)
- Reply to comments and DMs
- Engage with 10-20 accounts in your niche
- Post 1-2 Stories
