Exchange flows are the signal most people miss.

→ Crypto moving TO exchanges = preparing to sell (bearish)
→ Crypto moving OUT = taking custody, holding (bullish)

In 2022, massive stablecoin inflows to exchanges flagged the crash WEEKS before price caught up.
